About

The Mine Rescue Center will be a resource for mine rescue professionals across the world. While this is a major undertaking, it'll start small and grow into something of value to others.

Minimal Viable Product

Initially, we're focusing on the U.S. mine rescue scene and MSHA's rules and competitions. Our goal is to create something that can be run sustainably, with high quality, and useful to mine rescue professionals.

  • Landing Page - attached to domain minerescuecenter.com
  • Problem Repository - all old problems found from the wayback machine on miningquiz.com, with contact from those responsible for competitions since miningquiz.com went down
  • Competition Calendar - all mine rescue competitions with locations. Click on an event to see its information. Upon completion, results and the problem are posted here (starting in 2026, with historical data added as time allows)
